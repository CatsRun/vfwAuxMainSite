Come and join us as we give service to our community and those who sacrificed to protect it.

Who is eligable to join?

The Veterans of Foreign Wars Auxiliary is for spouse, parents, children, grandchildren and siblings of veterans who have are eligible for membership in the Veterans of Foreign Wars of the United States.
These incluse service members who have received a campaign medal for overseas service or have served 30 consecutive or 60 non-consecutive days in Korea, or have ever received hostile fire or imminent danger pay.

How do I join?

You can join by completing an Application and bringing it to our next meeting. Meeting are held the 3rd Tuseday of the month at:

Hopyard Farm Clubhouse, 5888 Coakley Drive, King George.
Dinner start 5:30
Meeting starts 6:30
